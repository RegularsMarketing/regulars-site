How We Work

We're pre-launch — so we lead with the process, not manufactured proof.

No fake testimonials, no borrowed case studies. Here's exactly what happens, in order, when you work with Regulars.

01

Free Discovery Call

A 20-minute conversation about your restaurant, what's already working, and where first-timers are slipping through the cracks. No pitch deck.

02

Audit & Baseline

We pull your starting numbers — Google views, calls, direction requests, review count and rating, social following, list size — before anything changes, so results are provable, not claimed.

03

Onboarding Build

We set up the systems: Google Business Profile, review flow, content calendar, and — for Full House — your website and POS-connected list. Full House builds go live within 21 days.

04

Launch

Everything goes live together, so your local visibility, reputation, and retention channels work from the same playbook from day one.

05

Grow — the monthly cycle

Reviews answered within 48 hours. Reports by the 5th business day of every month, in plain English, measured against your baseline. One human owns your account.

That's the whole process. Nothing hidden behind a "schedule a call to learn more."
